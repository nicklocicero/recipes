Ingredients:
 - quinoa
 - greens
 - tahini sauce
 - lemon
 - garlic
 - olive oil
 - parsley
 - kim chi
 - sea greens

Directions:
  1. make the quinoa
  2. blend tahini sauce, lemon, garlic, olive oil, and parsley
  3. stir in a bowl the quinoa, greens, tahini blend, and kim chi
  4. serve with sea greens garnish
